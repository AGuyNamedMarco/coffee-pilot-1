## Objective
Define a lightweight visual direction for the Alcove homepage concept so implementation supports a marketable, community-centered coffee brand.

## Brand Direction
Warm, local, human, community-centered, approachable.

## Visual Strategy
Shift from transactional utility toward neighborhood belonging while preserving clear ordering actions.

## Color Direction
Primary:
- Warm cream backgrounds
- Deep espresso text
- Soft coffee brown surfaces

Accent:
- Muted golden yellow for calls to action
- Warm terracotta or clay for emphasis

Avoid:
- Cold grays
- Overly corporate neutrals
- Generic black-and-white minimalism

## Typography Direction
Use strong hierarchy:
- Large, warm editorial-style headline
- Simple readable body text
- Clear button labels

Tone:
- Human
- Clear
- Not overly clever

## Layout Direction
Use generous spacing, large sections, and clear narrative flow.

Homepage rhythm:
Belonging -> Story -> Community proof -> Order -> Rewards -> Visit

## Imagery Direction
Prioritize authentic photography:
- People gathering
- Outdoor coffee truck
- Real customers
- Community moments

Avoid:
- Generic stock coffee imagery
- Over-polished lifestyle visuals that feel detached from the business

## Component Direction
Core components:
- Hero
- Story block
- Community proof cards
- Featured drinks
- Rewards panel
- Visit card
- Footer

## Accessibility Direction
- Maintain readable contrast
- Use semantic headings
- Keep CTAs visually clear
- Avoid image-only communication

## Reusable Pattern Candidate
Community-first coffee homepage.
