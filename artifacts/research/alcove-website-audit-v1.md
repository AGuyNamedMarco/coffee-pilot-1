## UX Findings
Strengths:
- Key conversion actions are present: order pickup, full menu, gift cards, loyalty program.
- Hours and address are visible.
- Instagram link supports social discovery.

Weaknesses:
- The extracted content does not communicate a clear brand story or value proposition beyond coffee ordering.
- The page appears oriented around transactional actions rather than explaining why Alcove is distinct.
- Community positioning is implied by the name but not strongly developed in visible text.

## Conversion Findings
Primary conversion paths appear to be:
- Order pickup
- View full menu
- Buy gift cards
- Start loyalty rewards
- Follow on Instagram

Opportunity:
Clarify the primary conversion priority. If the business goal is visits, ordering, loyalty, or community engagement, the website should prioritize that path more deliberately.

## SEO Observations
Strengths:
- Page title includes service, neighborhood, city, and brand.
- Meta description includes coffee truck and product categories.

Opportunities:
- Add richer local content around Eastlake, Chula Vista, coffee truck service, community events, and customer experience.
- Add structured content that explains Alcove's story, offerings, service model, and location context.

## Accessibility Observations
Not enough evidence for a confident accessibility assessment. Screenshot and DOM review are needed for color contrast, heading structure, alt text, and keyboard navigation assumptions.

## Risks
- Current website may undersell the brand if it is primarily transactional.
- Community differentiation may be underutilized.
- Visual/aesthetic conclusions remain low confidence without screenshot inspection.
- If the site relies heavily on Square defaults, customization and SEO control may be limited.

## Opportunities
- Strengthen homepage positioning around outdoor coffee, community, and local Eastlake identity.
- Add a concise story section explaining who Alcove serves and why it exists.
- Promote loyalty and gift cards as retention tools.
- Build a clearer hierarchy around ordering, visiting, and following.
- Develop a reusable coffee-industry website pattern from this pilot.

## Unknowns
- Actual visual design quality
- Mobile layout quality
- Image quality and authenticity
- Owner goals
- Primary business objective
- Competitor positioning
