# Observation Guidelines

artifact_id: REF-OBS-001
purpose: Define evidence standards for analyzing websites.

## Institutional Rule
No aesthetic or UX claim without observable evidence.

Bad:
"Feels premium"

Good:
"Hero occupies ~70% viewport height, CTA count=1, whitespace density high, product imagery dominant"

---

## Capture Requirements
For every reference collect:

### 1. Viewports
- mobile screenshot
- tablet screenshot
- desktop screenshot

Record:
- hero height
- visible CTAs
- navigation complexity

---
