# Quality Taxonomy

artifact_id: QOS-002
purpose: Define the observable causes of perceived quality.

## Trust
Increased by:
- Real photography
- Founder presence
- Testimonials/reviews
- Consistent branding
- Transparent pricing/contact
Destroyed by:
- Stock imagery
- Broken links
- Generic copy

## Emotion
Increased by:
- Strong imagery
- Storytelling
- Aspirational framing
- Sense of place
Destroyed by:
- Utility-only presentation
- Overcrowding

## Authenticity
Increased by:
- Local context
- Imperfect but real visuals
- Community references
Destroyed by:
- AI genericity
- Corporate language

## Premium Perception
Increased by:
- Whitespace
- Typography restraint
- Consistency
- High quality photography
Destroyed by:
- Clutter
- Excessive colors

## Hierarchy
Increased by:
- Clear primary action
- Strong contrast
- Predictable layout
Destroyed by:
- Competing priorities

## Conversion
Increased by:
- Immediate CTA
- Reduced friction
- Social proof
Destroyed by:
- Hidden actions
- Excess cognitive load

## Industry Modifiers
Coffee/Hospitality:
 prioritize:
  - warmth
  - authenticity
  - locality
  - atmosphere
 deprioritize:
  - corporate polish

## Evaluation Rule
A score must reference observable evidence.
Bad: 'Feels weak'
Good: 'Authenticity reduced by stock imagery and absent local story'
