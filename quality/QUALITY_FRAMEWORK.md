# Quality Operating System (QOS)

artifact_id: QOS-001
status: draft
purpose: Define how the institution evaluates website quality.

## Thesis
The institution competes on repeatable high-quality judgment, not automation alone.

## Quality Dimensions (1–10)

### Trust
1 = suspicious/template
10 = immediately credible
Signals:
- authentic imagery
- clarity
- reviews
- founder presence

### Emotion
1 = forgettable
10 = evokes desire/belonging

### Authenticity
1 = generic AI/template
10 = unmistakably local/real

### Hierarchy
1 = confusing
10 = obvious information flow

### Premium Perception
1 = cheap
10 = high-end/professional

### Clarity
1 = confusing
10 = immediate understanding

### Memorability
1 = interchangeable
10 = distinct identity

### Conversion
1 = weak action
10 = clear next step

### Responsiveness
1 = broken mobile
10 = excellent across devices

## Benchmarks
0–4: low quality template
5–6: average small business
7–8: strong agency work
9–10: exceptional / category leading

## Institutional Rules
1. Never ship after one concept.
2. Compare variants.
3. Authenticity > polish for local business.
4. Human feedback becomes memory.
5. Every subsystem must prove measurable quality improvement.
