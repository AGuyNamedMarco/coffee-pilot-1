# Quality Benchmarks

artifact_id: QOS-003
purpose: Define external standards the institution compares against.

## Benchmark Levels

### Level 1 — Commodity / Generic
Examples:
- default Wix templates
- low effort local business sites

Characteristics:
- interchangeable
- weak hierarchy
- low trust

Target score:
0–4

---

### Level 2 — Competent / Average
Examples:
- average agency sites
- competent small business redesigns

Characteristics:
- clear
- usable
- forgettable

Target score:
5–6

---

### Level 3 — Strong / Professional
Examples:
- respected agencies
- thoughtful hospitality brands

Characteristics:
- memorable
- emotionally aware
- cohesive

Target score:
7–8

---

### Level 4 — Exceptional / Category Leading
Examples:
- Airbnb
- Apple
- premium hospitality brands
- leading creative agencies

Characteristics:
- immediate emotional response
- distinct identity
- exceptional clarity
- trust
- aspiration

Target score:
9–10

---

## Institutional Rule
Outputs must be compared against:
1. Similar industry
2. Best in class examples
3. Prior institutional outputs
